IndusVale

“ As firm believers in the 'Make in India' movement, we want to set the standards not just for 'Make in India' but also for 'Innovate in India’ ”

IndusVale, found its origin as the brainchild of a group of individuals that found their passion is disrupting the idea of lifestyle from the lenses of an Indian brand. A brand that conveys the picture of India from what was, what is, and what it ought to be. Adopting the name from Indus Valley Civilization, IndusVale is about the people, and how they express themselves in their colors, in their clothing, in their lifestyle, how they connect, how they do business, and everything under the sun.

So when IndusVale wanted to develop apparels and lifestyle products, we understood that there were two ends to it. One was the side seen in fashion shows, not robust, very delicate, cannot be worn everyday. The other was our everyday side, that had not seen any innovation in design or in utility in decades. And we decided to change the landscape. Introducing AI to innovate in our designs, and research for patent development in utility and material itself.
