IndusVale

“

As firm believers in the 'Make in India' movement, we want to set the standards not just for 'Make in India' but also for 'Innovate in India’

IndusVale, found its origin as the brainchild of Indians passionate about disrupting the idea of lifestyle from the lenses of a futuristic Indian brand. A brand that conveys the picture of India from what was, what is, and what it ought to be. Adopting the name from Indus Valley Civilization, IndusVale is about the people, and how they express themselves in their colors, in their clothing, in their lifestyle, how they connect, how they do business, and everything that makes their life worthwhile.

So when IndusVale wants to develop lifestype products, we understand that we need to have keen focus on bringing innovative products. We want to change the landscape in design and manufacturing when we bring a platform to our people. We do this by introducing artificial intelligence as a key component in bringing design innovation and continuous focus on patent development for the utility and materials of products themselves.
